Our Story

On a Crisp night in october we were taking our daily evening stroll through the woods and noticed more kids in the woods. We heard screams of dispair and felt an unrest, so we thought someone was in trouble. So we panicked and went towards the noise, only to see that in a strange turn of events, they were infact running away from us

As a band of brothers and sisters, we've come together to provide safespaces for our own. We understand the hardships and after years of Hollywood Appropriation and misrepresentation, we need our own space.

As 3 lifelong clowns, we knew being us... can be difficult. So we've worked together with other persecuted groups to share land that we can get kicked off any second - year around. You can be yourself here - until they find us
Welcome home. Camping for Clowns.
