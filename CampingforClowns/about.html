Our Story

On a Crisp night in october we were taking our daily knife sharpening stroll to our cabin in the woods, when we suddenly heard screams. It was here Ronny Mcdonald, Sideshows Bobby and I (Krustee the Krown) noticed it was us who these kids were runnning from

As a band of brothers and sisters, we've come together to see that we are underrepresented in all walks of life. We understand the hardships and after years of Hollywood Appropriation and misrepresentation, we realized we were in need of our own space.

As 3 avid clowns for over 20 years, we knew being ourselves aint easy. Especially during this current social and political landscape. Nothing is given and people are fearing that which they dont understand more than ever. We must stand our ground.

So we've worked together with other persecuted groups to share land that we can get kicked off any second - year around. You can be yourself here - until they find us.
